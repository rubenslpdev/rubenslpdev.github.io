My name is Rubens LP, I'm an UX/UI designer, web developer & illustrator from São Paulo - Brazil.

Available for hire

With over two decades of experience bridging creativity and technology, I've dedicated my career to crafting intuitive, user-centered digital experiences that merge aesthetics with functionality.

From my early beginnings in the late 90s—when I built my first website, FluxusCentral, during my Industrial Design studies at Mackenzie University—to my current focus on UX/UI design, art and technology have always been intertwined in my work.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of collaborating with prestigious brands such as Adidas, Nike, Absolut Vodka, Coca-Cola, Microsoft, MTV, Rede Globo, Ed. Abril, Sony Ericsson, and Veuve Clicquot. These experiences have honed my ability to transform complex challenges into elegant solutions, balancing rigorous user-centered methodologies—like usability testing, empathy mapping, design systems, and rapid prototyping—with strong visual design sensibilities.

In 2020, I deepened my expertise in UX design and web development, complementing my extensive background with certifications like the Google UX Design Professional Certificate and the Responsive Web Design program from freeCodeCamp. Recently, my participation in the Bitcoin UX Design Bootcamp expanded my horizons, allowing me to explore the unique challenges and opportunities within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. During the Bootcamp, I contributed to Mostro, a Lightning P2P platform that runs on the Nostr protocol, doing usability tests and UX benchmarks for its mobile application.
